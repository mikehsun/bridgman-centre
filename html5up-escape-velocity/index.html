Who We Are

Our story

The Bridgman Centre is a community centre dedicated to educating and supporting residents in Zola, Soweto.

In 1975, the United Congressional Church of Southern Africa opened the Bridgman Development Centre in Zola, Soweto. The centre was built to provide educational support and leadership development to high school students in the community. The first students came from a world riddled with poverty, discrimination, and despair. But despite the challenges of living through the system of apartheid, the Bridgman Centre has always committed itself to help individuals and the community achieve success.
